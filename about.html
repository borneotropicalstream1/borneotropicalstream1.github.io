About us

WHO WE ARE

Borneo Tropical Stream is a premium tilapia farming company based in South Borneo, Indonesia. Specializing in cultivating and farming Borneo rainforest tilapia. Our mission is to provide the freshest, tastiest, and highest-quality tilapia while preserving and protecting its ecosystem.

We believe in sustainable and responsible business practices, and our goal is to provide our customers with a healthy source of protein that is not only delicious but ethically and environmentally sound. By operating with integrity and transparency, we aim to establish ourselves as a trusted and respected provider of tilapia in the world.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and our commitment to animal welfare and environmental protection allow us to produce tilapia that not only tastes great, but is also free from harmful additives and contaminants. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality tilapia to our customers and strive to continuously improve our methods and practices to ensure that our product is the best it can be.

With our vision of becoming a leading provider of sustainably-raised, high-quality tilapia in Indonesia, we are poised for growth and success in the years to come. We look forward to exporting our product to customers worldwide and sharing our passion for delicious, sustainable, and responsible tilapia with the world.

VISION

To be a leading provider of sustainably-raised, high-quality tilapia in Indonesia, while preserving and protecting its ecosystem.

MISSION

Our mission is to create a sustainable and responsible business that produces the freshest and tastiest tilapia while preserving the rainforest and its ecosystem. We strive to provide our customers with a healthy and delicious source of protein, while maintaining the highest standards of environmental responsibility and animal welfare. By operating with integrity and transparency, we aim to establish ourselves as a trusted and respected provider of tilapia in Indonesia.
